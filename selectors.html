HITMAN ROHIT SHARMA

Welcome to the world of Rohit Sharma!

Rohit Sharma is an Indian international cricketer and the current captain of the Indian national cricket team in limited-overs formats. He is widely regarded as one of the best batsmen in the world.

Achievements: ODI World Cup winner, Multiple IPL titles with Mumbai Indians, Record holder for fastest century in T20Is,Highest individual score in ODI.

Known for his elegant cover drives and powerful hitting, Rohit has scored over 10,000 runs in international cricket.
